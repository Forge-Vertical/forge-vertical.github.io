Forge Vertical Security PolicyForge Vertical is built on a foundation of integrity. We believe in proactive research and the collective intelligence of the security community.Supported VersionsWe only provide security updates for the current production version of our infrastructure.VersionSupported1.x:white_check_mark:< 1.0:x:Our CommitmentWe take security seriously. If you find a vulnerability, we promise to:Acknowledge your report within 24 hours.Provide a timeline for resolution and keep you informed.Offer Safe Harbor: We will not take legal action against researchers who act in good faith and follow this policy.Reporting a VulnerabilityDo not open a public issue. Please use one of the following secure channels:GitHub Private Reporting: Click the "Security" tab and select "Report a vulnerability" (Recommended).Email: Send your findings to jarrit@forgevertical.com.What to IncludeTo help us triage and reward faster, please include:A clear description of the vulnerability.A Proof of Concept (PoC) or steps to reproduce.An assessment of the potential impact (e.g., Logic Flaw, Auth Bypass).Rules of EngagementAvoid privacy violations, destruction of data, and interruption or degradation of our service.Only interact with accounts you own or with explicit permission from the account holder.Do not perform Denial of Service (DoS) attacks on production infrastructure.Note to ResearchersForge Vertical is currently in its foundational phase. While we do not offer monetary rewards or "Repute" points at this immediate moment, we are building a high-integrity ecosystem designed for the world's most critical infrastructure.If you are interested in joining our future managed programs as an elite partner, please register your interest here:Apply for Forge Vertical Hacker WaitlistThank you for helping us build the next generation of secure infrastructure.
