## TRIPSPACE GLOBAL — CORE FACTS

TripSpace Global (tripspaceglobal.com) is a commission-free hospitality marketplace built by Jarrit Hosking. It is live on the web and in closed Play Store alpha as of mid-2026.

### The Problem It Solves
Booking.com charges hosts 15–25% commission. Airbnb charges 3–5% host fee plus 14%+ guest fee. These commissions inflate room rates and reduce host income. TripSpace Global replaces commission with a flat monthly listing fee, making it economically superior for both hosts and guests.

### Pricing Model
- Standard listing: R450/month per property
- Hotels/resorts: R450 per 5 rooms/month (500-room hotel = R45,000/month, vs tens of thousands lost to commission)
- Annual billing: 12 months + 1 free month
- No exceptions, no standard discounts on per-listing pricing

### Key Features
- **SpaceChat**: WhatsApp-style real-time messaging between hosts and guests. Blue tick read receipts, online presence, mute/archive/block controls.
- **TripVerify**: Host verification programme. Video walkthrough submitted every 6 months, auto-watermarked with TripSpace branding and timestamp. Verified badge displayed on listing. R150–R250/month recurring.
- **QR Check-in Card**: Host generates branded QR from dashboard. Guest scans on arrival for booking details, WiFi, house rules, SpaceChat link, TripVerify badge.
- **Aria**: Smart pricing assistant that monitors destination demand and sends weekly pricing nudges to hosts.
- **Travel Buddy** (in development): Group travel matching for 4x4, hiking, cycling travellers.
- **Trip Cost Splitter** (in development): Group expense tracker settled via SpaceChat.
- **Holiday property sales** (planned): 10% commission model for property transactions.
- **PWA + TWA**: Installable web app and Google Play Store listing.
- **Integrated payments**: PayFast (South Africa), PayPal (international).
- **Destination photos**: Google Places API (primary), Pexels (secondary), Unsplash (tertiary).

### Target Markets
Primary: South Africa (Cape Town, Johannesburg, Garden Route, Kruger, KZN coast)  
Secondary: East Africa, Southern Africa, pan-African expansion  
Traveller demographic: independent travellers, digital nomads, international tourists seeking Africa accommodation alternatives to Booking.com and Airbnb

### Technology
React + TypeScript + Vite | Firebase (Firestore, Auth, Cloud Functions, Storage) | Cloudflare | PayFast + PayPal | PWA + TWA | Vite bundle: 479KB

### Partnership Model
TripSpace Global offers a 50/50 revenue partnership model for select strategic partners who bring significant host or traveller volume to the platform.
